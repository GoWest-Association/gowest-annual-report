Federal Advocacy

Public Engagement

Activated thousands of credit union members to contact Congressional delegations in opposition to Interchange Expansion.

Securing Legislation

Advocated for passage of Credit Union Board Modernization legislation in U.S. House, with 12 co-sponsors on House and Senate Bills.

Strengthening Allies

Secured Senator Kyrsten Sinema (D-AZ) as original sponsor of Senate version of the Credit Union Board Modernization Bill.

Enlightening Policymakers

Met with 44 out of 48 members of our Congressional delegations, to advance public policy issues that impact credit unions.

Funding Support

Provided PAC support for and/or hosted in-person fundraising events for 33 members of our delegations.

State Advocacy

Arizona

Protected credit unions’ tax exemption and defeated potentially burdensome ESG and vehicle repossession legislation. Launched first Governmental Affairs Committee and hosted twenty candidate meetings at credit unions across Arizona.

Colorado

Protected credit unions’ tax exemption and supported legislation incentivizing lending to low-income borrowers. Launched new Governmental Affairs Committee and hosted State Issues Working Group to develop policy modernization recommendations.

Idaho

With input from State Issues Working Group, passed legislation modernizing the Credit Union Act, passed the Idaho Digital Asset Act to enhance technology, advocated against burdensome ESG legislation, and connected CU executives with incoming legislators.

Oregon

Protected credit unions’ tax exemption, supported additional funds for Individual Development Accounts, guarded credit unions’ best interests in harmful data broker legislation, distributed $175,000 in PAC funds and met with over 120 state legislative candidates.

Washington

Passed charter modernization legislation with bipartisan support, creating nation-leading investment authority enabling credit unions to partner with fintechs, expand services to non-members, provide relief to small credit unions and removing outdated regulations.

Wyoming

Protected credit unions’ tax exemption, launched first-ever Governmental Affairs Committee, and advocated for candidates who support credit union priorities, including legislation to allow credit unions to hold public funds.

Grassroots Advocacy

2,500+ unique points of engagement between legislators and credit union advocates to advance public policy agenda.

100+ credit union advocates received training in advocacy to empower them to advance credit union interests.

Developed better tools to strengthen contacts with legislators, overcoming challenges of re-districting and staff turnover.

Identified advocacy coordinators to champion public policy initiatives and advocacy within their credit union.

Regulatory Advocacy

NEV Changes

Led effort to change parameters of NEV supervisory test and eliminate requirement to issue DOR if credit union falls into extreme risk category

Equity Investment

Leading efforts advocating for the NCUA to adopt rulemaking that would make broad equity investments permissible

CECL Implementation

Advocated for NCUA to release a CECL implementation tool for small credit unions which was made available in October

Reducing NOL

Continued advocacy effort to lower the NOL to 3% which could result in a SIF distribution if adopted

Corporate Resolution

Advocated for timely payments from the Corporate System Resolution Program
