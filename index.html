Federal Advocacy

Data-Driven Approach

Collaborated with industry partners to develop research and data sets to educate policymakers and support credit union advocacy positions.

Educating Policymakers

Met with 47 out of 48 members of our GoWest Congressional delegation to advance public policy issues that impact credit unions.

Securing Legislation

Successfully advocated for and influenced legislation in Congress including Credit Union Board Modernization, SAFER Banking, CDFI transparency, MBL flexibility, etc.

Leveraging GoWest Leadership

Engaged Congressional leaders and secured original bill sponsors Crapo, Lummis, Merkley, Pettersen and Sinema to advance credit union priority legislation.

Funding Support

Provided PAC support for and/or hosted in-person fundraising events for 34 members of our delegations.

State Advocacy

Arizona

  • Defeated all anti-ESG legislation.
  • Safeguarded liability protections for credit unions as lienholders.
  • Protected credit union interests in record-keeping requirements.
  • Convened first State Issues Work Group to create a future-focused legislative agenda.

Colorado

  • Engaged in the sunset process to modernize the Credit Union Act.
  • Developed strategic relationships with legislative leadership, committee chairpersons, and new legislators.
  • Protected credit union interests in lending, GAP insurance legislation.
  • Defeated all anti-ESG legislation.

Idaho

  • Stopped interchange legislation.
  • Protected credit unions from liability risks on MCC gun code legislation. Category Codes Bill.
  • Passed financial education requirements legislation (HB 92).
  • Convened State Issues Work Group to create a future-focused legislative agenda

Oregon

  • Passed significant Credit Union Act modernization.
  • Passed legislation in support of electronic titling.
  • Passed bipartisan financial education bill.
  • Protected credit union interests in data privacy, state bank, and garnishment legislation.

Washington

  • Initiated and advanced negotiations with Washington Department of Revenue on evolving sales tax interpretation with significant potential impact.
  • Protected credit union interests in robocalling, personal information disclosure, lien priority, and foreclosure legislation.
  • Maintained funding for Credit Building Pilot Program grant in state budget.

Wyoming

  • Prevented harmful gun merchant code legislation.
  • Supported elder abuse prevention legislation.
  • Limited the homestead exemption to $50,000.
  • Protected credit unions in ESG conversation.

Regulatory Advocacy

Successful Advocacy with the NCUA to Improve the Credit Union Operating Environment

Engaged and helped shape rules related to expanding field of membership authority, member expulsion, expanded loan participation, and improved mortgage servicing account treatment.

Ensured GoWest Member Participation on CFPB Rulemaking

Led effort that resulted in successful appointments to the Credit Union Advisory Committee and ensured representation on SBREFA panels.

Colorado Sunset Review Negotiations with DORA

Led successful advocacy efforts resulting in six legislative recommendations included in the Colorado Sunset review.

Reduced Assessments

Led effort to substantially reduce assessments on Oregon credit unions and are evaluating ways to reduce assessments on credit unions across the GoWest footprint
